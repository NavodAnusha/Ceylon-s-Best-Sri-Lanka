INSPIRATION

SRI LANKA, THE FORMER CEYLON



Sri Lanka - Ceylon was the official name of the island until 1972 - offers more than beach and ocean and more than just strongholds for package tourists.
Sri Lanka is an exotic island with a 2500 year old history, diverse religious culture, seductive nature and lush vegetation. Here you can experience wildlife adventures, colonial flair and plantation romance combined with golf in a special atmosphere.

The scents of the spice gardens and the oils of Ayurvedic naturopathy are a delight for all the senses. The kilometers of dream beaches, lined with palm trees, invite you not only to swim, but also to many adventures. Central Province The Central Province is a dream for nature lovers and the historical focus of Sri Lanka. Experience the country and people of Sri Lanka, the change between cloud forest vegetation, dry and warm humid areas and marvel at the wonderful natural beauties. Southern Province The southern province of Sri Lanka lets you feel the change between rainforest vegetation, dry zones and warm, humid areas.


Enjoy the natural beauties of living and past times and get to know the inhabitants of Sri Lanka up close.
